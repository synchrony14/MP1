What is Music Connect?

Music Connect is a website that envisions working remotely with other independent musicians, artists, song writers, instrumentalists, and other music production related preferences and needs as well.

This idea was created to help people who has the same passion for music but lacks time, knowledge, and resources to produce music by means of having a centralized website wherein they can upload and download files to satisfy their needs regarding their preferences.

Services of this website also include publishing of songs into different musical platforms such as Spotify, Amazon, Apple Music, and many more.

This will enable an effortless experience for many while doing their daily routines and stuff without having to worry about how to manage their time to make space for creating music, or even have the need to worry about on what proper equipment and gears to use.

Music Connect uses updated equipments, softwares, and high-end musical gears ready and fit for music production in all different genres and styles. That said, Music Connect is able to produce high audio quality sound and recording or commonly known as "Radio Mix" ready to be published and streamed.
